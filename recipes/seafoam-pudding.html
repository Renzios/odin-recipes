Seafoam Pudding

Description

This briny pudding will really get you into the maritime mindset!

Ingredients

  • Flounder (1)
  • Midnight Carp (1)
  • Squid Ink (1)

Steps

  1. Obtain the recipe by reaching Fishing (Level 9)
  2. Prepare using either the kitchen inside an upgraded farmhouse or a Cookout Kit.
